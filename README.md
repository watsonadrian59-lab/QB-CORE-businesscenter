# QB-CORE-businesscenter
This script allows players in your city to buy and sell popular businesses! Config, allows for multiple businesses to be sold at your desired location, players can then manage their business from the same tab upon purchase!


INSTALLATION:

Download the file.

Extract the file.

Drag into [standalone] OR [misc] folders.

Restart and ensure the resource.

Enjoy!



Dependencies:

- QB-CORE
- QB-INPUT
- QB-MENU
